AI Governance, Law & Policy

Var Shankar

Var Shankar is an attorney and AI policy adviser who works with large organizations on complex regulatory, privacy and AI matters. He leads the Council on AI Governance and teaches AI governance at Purdue University. Var previously practiced law at Cravath, Swaine & Moore LLP and worked on the Clinton Global Initiative. He is a graduate of Harvard Law School.
